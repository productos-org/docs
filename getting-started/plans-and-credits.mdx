ProductOS uses one pricing catalog across every surface. One account and one plan work across Build, Code, and Design, and your credits are shared, so there is no per-surface math.

## Credits

Credits power every AI generation, from concept to code. A few things to know:

- **Cost scales with the model.** A call to a fast, lightweight model consumes far fewer credits than a call to a top-tier reasoning model.
- **You see estimates up front.** ProductOS provides a cost preview before you commit to an action, and the final charge is calculated from the actual tokens used.
- **A typical feature costs about 20 to 25 credits end to end**, across the whole pipeline.
- **Running out is not a wall.** You can purchase additional credits a la carte or upgrade to a higher plan, and you are notified as you approach your limit.

<Tip>
  If you bring your own API keys, runs on your keys skip credit deduction entirely. See [BYOK and models](/platform/byok-and-models).
</Tip>

## Individual plans

Individual plans bill per workspace.

| Plan | Price | Credits |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Hobby** | Free | 30 / mo |
| **Pro** | $20 / mo | 100 / mo |
| **Pro+** | $60 / mo | 300 / mo |
| **Ultra** | $200 / mo | 1,000 / mo |

- **Hobby** is free forever: build with a live preview, publish a live link, fork and remix templates. No credit card required.
- **Pro** adds a custom domain with no watermark, web, mobile, desktop and agent targets, unlimited private projects, all frontier models, and GitHub sync with 1-click deploy.
- **Pro+** is everything in Pro with 3x the credits and higher build concurrency.
- **Ultra** adds the highest build concurrency, system prompt editing, and priority chat support.

Annual billing gives you 2 months free (annual = monthly x 10). On annual billing the per-month equivalents are $17 for Pro, $50 for Pro+, and $167 for Ultra.

## Business plans

Team and Agency bill per active user, with a pooled monthly credit allotment for the whole workspace. Seats adjust automatically with proration as members join or leave.

| Plan | Price | Credits |
|------|-------|---------|
| **Team** | $40 / user / mo | 750 / mo, pooled |
| **Agency** | $120 / user / mo | 4,500 / mo, pooled |

- **Team** adds centralized billing and admin, roles and permissions, a shared team workspace, shared skills and plugins, usage analytics, and SAML/OIDC SSO.
- **Agency** is everything in Team plus white-label and resell rights, up to 5 client workspaces, custom branding, and dedicated support.

For larger needs, Enterprise plans are scoped case by case: contact [enterprise@productos.dev](mailto:enterprise@productos.dev).

## Billing details

- Paid plans include a 14-day free trial, and new subscribers get a 14-day money-back guarantee.
- You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the end of the billing cycle.
- Payments are handled through Polar and all major credit cards are accepted.
- Older Pro/Max/Business plans are grandfathered and fully supported in-app.

<Note>
  Pricing details can change. For the current catalog and full plan comparison, see [productos.dev/pricing](https://productos.dev/pricing).
</Note>
